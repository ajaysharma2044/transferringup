# Google Meet Auto-Creation Setup Guide

## 🎯 How to Enable Automatic Google Meet Creation

Your Google Calendar embed is already working, but to ensure Google Meet links are automatically created for every booking, follow these steps:

### Step 1: Configure Your Google Calendar Settings

1. **Go to Google Calendar**: https://calendar.google.com
2. **Click the gear icon** (Settings) in the top right
3. **Select "Settings"** from the dropdown

### Step 2: Enable Google Meet for All Events

1. **In Settings**, click **"Event settings"** on the left sidebar
2. **Find "Add Google Meet video conferencing"**
3. **Select one of these options**:
   - ✅ **"Add Google Meet video conferencing to all events I create"** (RECOMMENDED)
   - ✅ **"Ask to add Google Meet video conferencing to events I create"**

### Step 3: Configure Your Appointment Schedule

1. **Go back to Google Calendar main view**
2. **Find your appointment schedule** (the one you embedded)
3. **Click on it** to edit
4. **In the appointment settings**:
   - ✅ **Check "Add Google Meet video conferencing"**
   - ✅ **Set default meeting duration** (recommend 60 minutes)
   - ✅ **Add meeting description template**

1. **Book a test appointment** using your embedded calendar

When someone books through your calendar, they should automatically get:

1. **📧 Email confirmation** with:

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### If Google Meet Links Aren't Auto-Creating:

1. **Check Calendar Settings**:
   - Ensure "Add Google Meet" is enabled
   - Verify you're the calendar owner
   - Check that appointments are set to create events

2. **Verify Permissions**:
   - Make sure your Google account has Meet access
   - Check that calendar sharing is set correctly
   - Ensure appointment schedule is published

3. **Test Different Browsers**:
   - Try booking in Chrome, Firefox, Safari
   - Clear browser cache and cookies
   - Disable ad blockers temporarily

4. **Check Email Settings**:
   - Verify notification settings in Google Calendar
   - Check spam/junk folders
   - Ensure calendar invitations are enabled

### Common Issues & Solutions:

**Issue**: "Meet link not appearing in calendar event"
**Solution**: 
- Edit the appointment schedule
- Re-enable "Add Google Meet video conferencing"
- Save and test again

**Issue**: "Students not receiving calendar invitations"
**Solution**:
- Check your calendar sharing settings
- Ensure "Send invitations" is enabled
- Verify email addresses are correct

**Issue**: "Multiple Meet links being created"
**Solution**:
- Don't manually add Meet links
- Let Google auto-generate them
- Remove duplicate events
